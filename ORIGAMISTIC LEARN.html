FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEONS

3. Chameleons like warm and tropical environments like the rain forest.

4. Unlike many lizards, chameleon can't regrow their tails.

3. The world's first TEDDY BEAR museum was set up in Petersfield, Hampshire, England in 1984.

4. The smallest teddy bear that ever sold was only 0.29 inches tall..
